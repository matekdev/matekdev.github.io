Parkour Painters is a multiplayer parkour game where gangs race around the city and cover territory with graffiti. It won **$1600 USD** during an s&box game jam.

I built the parkour movement system, including wall jumps, ledge grabs, and grinding. That was the core feel of the game, so I spent a lot of time tuning movement and making sure it behaved properly in multiplayer. I also worked on gameplay systems, designed the UI, and made one of the music tracks used in the game.
